Frequently Asked Questions About YouTube Downloaders

Is it legal to download YouTube videos?

Downloading YouTube videos for personal offline use is generally considered acceptable in most countries, though it is technically against YouTube's Terms of Service. Downloading copyrighted content and redistributing it or using it commercially is illegal and against copyright law. Always use downloaded videos for personal use only and respect the rights of content creators.

Does yt1bet work for private YouTube videos?

No. yt1bet can only download publicly accessible YouTube videos. Private videos and unlisted videos that require authentication cannot be downloaded.

Why is my download opening in a new tab instead of saving?

On some browsers, especially on mobile, video files open in a new tab before downloading. Simply long-press the video and select "Save video" or "Download" from your browser's menu to save it to your device.

What is the difference between YouTube to MP4 and YouTube to MP3?

MP4 is a video file format — it contains both the video and audio. MP3 is an audio-only format — it contains just the sound track from the video, with no video. Use MP4 to keep the full video. Use MP3 if you only need the audio (music, podcasts, lectures).

Can I download YouTube playlists?

Currently yt1bet processes one video at a time. For bulk playlist downloads, you would need a dedicated desktop application. For single video downloads, yt1bet is the fastest free option available.

How long does it take to download a YouTube video?

Most videos process and start downloading within 5 to 15 seconds. Longer videos at higher resolutions (1080p) may take a bit longer to process, but the download itself depends on your internet connection speed.
